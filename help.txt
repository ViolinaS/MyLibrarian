<Add New Book> - opens field with entries to add a new book into database.
<Delete Books> - opens Menu with list of all books in database to choose and delete
<Find a Book> - opens Menu with list of all books in database and lets to check each book details,
and add a book to Favorite list.
<Favorites> - shows the list\n of all favorite books in database with possibility to delete
books from favorites.
<Export to XLSX> - exports all books from database to XLSX file."
